# Fake SID v3.0 Release Notes

## What's New

- **Modern file access** - No more storage permissions needed! Uses Storage Access Framework (SAF)
- **IMPORT button** - Load songs from anywhere (Downloads, Google Drive, etc.)
- **SAVE AS button** - Export songs to any location
- **Export audio** (WAV/OGG) directly to your preferred location
- **Open from WhatsApp** - Open `.sng` files directly from WhatsApp, email, or other apps
- **Share to Fake SID** - Share `.sng` files from other apps directly to Fake SID

## Migration from v2.0

**Old songs are in:** `/fakesid/songs/`

**New location:** `/Android/data/com.twobit.fakesid/files/songs/`

### How to migrate your old songs:

1. Connect your phone to PC via USB
2. Choose "File Transfer" mode on your phone
3. Navigate to: `Internal Storage/fakesid/songs/`
4. Copy all `.sng` files
5. Paste to: `Internal Storage/Android/data/com.twobit.fakesid/files/songs/`
6. Open Fake SID - your songs will appear in the list!
